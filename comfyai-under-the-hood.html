ComfyAI under the hood

A microservice production platform for Comfy customer support: 5 AI agents, 6 microservices, a self-learning classifier — and a meta-twist: the system was built through the AI Workflow platform. AI making AI.

What it is

The signature trick: the system teaches itself. When classifier confidence drops, an AI generation of new keywords is triggered through Amazon Nova Micro — validated and saved to MongoDB. No engineer in the loop.

The interesting parts

Self-learning, routing, guardrails

Self-learning classifier

5 types of smart triggers (low confidence, ambiguous keyword, intent mismatch…) fire background learning through Nova Micro — new keywords are generated, validated and stored without blocking the user or waking an engineer.

Pattern routing with priorities

9 regex patterns with priorities 70–110: Gift, Return, Personal, Purchase, Greeting, Catalog, Characteristics, Analog, Reviews. Deterministic first, LLM second — cheap and predictable where possible.

Anti-hallucination guardrails

FAQ KB guardrails against LLM confabulation (“COMFY doesn’t sell iPhones”-class errors): a content audit of the knowledge base plus hard answer rules.

Contextual greeting v2

LLM greetings with localStorage persistence, guest→auth transition, skip-discovery via entity extraction and a 5-minute anti-spam cooldown.

The meta-story

AI making AI — and the BA contribution

Built through AI Workflow

Task artifacts for ComfyAI — BA specs, designs, tests — were generated by the PM/BA/Design/Dev/QA/Perf agents of AI Workflow, with skills-based review. The tool became a production product; the product was built by the tool.

Full-cycle BA work

Discovery, 57 NEEDs under Needs-Driven Development, MVP scope (5 agents instead of “everything at once”), dialogue UX and Quick Replies design. Several AI features piloted here are now being rolled into the Comfy e-com app.

Takeaway

What this case shows

A production multi-agent system is mostly not about the LLM: it’s deterministic routing before generation, guardrails around the knowledge base, self-learning that never blocks the user, and product features designed from real dialogue UX. The LLM is one component — the engineering around it is the platform.

Найцікавіше

Self-learning, роутинг, guardrails

Self-learning класифікатор

5 типів smart-тригерів (low confidence, ambiguous keyword, intent mismatch…) запускають фонове навчання через Nova Micro — нові ключові слова генеруються, валідуються і зберігаються, не блокуючи користувача і не будячи інженера.

Pattern Routing із пріоритетами

9 regex-патернів із пріоритетами 70–110: Gift, Return, Personal, Purchase, Greeting, Catalog, Characteristics, Analog, Reviews. Спершу детерміноване, потім LLM — дешево і передбачувано там, де можливо.

Anti-hallucination guardrails

FAQ KB guardrails проти вигадок LLM (помилки класу «COMFY не продає айфони»): аудит контенту бази знань плюс жорсткі правила відповідей.

Contextual Greeting v2

LLM-привітання з localStorage-персистентністю, Guest→Auth Transition, Skip Discovery через entity extraction і 5-хвилинний anti-spam cooldown.

Висновок

Що показує цей кейс

Production multi-agent система — це здебільшого не про LLM: це детермінований роутинг перед генерацією, guardrails навколо бази знань, self-learning, який не блокує користувача, і продуктові фічі, спроектовані від реального діалогового UX. LLM — один із компонентів; інженерія навколо нього і є платформою.
